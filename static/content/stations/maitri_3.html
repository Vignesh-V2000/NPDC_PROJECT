Maitri

Latitude: 70° 46.01' S (-70.766834°)

Longitude: 11° 43.847' E (11.730783°)

ESTD: 1989

Description: In the year 1988 an ice free, rocky area on the Schirmacher oasis was selected to build the second Research Station Maitri. The building was erected on steel stilts, and has since stood the test of time. It is an inland station about 100 km from the shore at an elevation of about 130 meters above sea level. Maitri Station can support 25 persons in the main building; in addition, during the summer, using containerized living modules, the station can accommodate about 40 persons. The station consists of one main building, fuel farm, fuel station, lake water pump house, a summer camp and a number of smaller containerized modules. * Note : Values matching for selected criteria are highlighted.
