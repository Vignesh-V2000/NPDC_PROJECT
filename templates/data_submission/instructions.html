Submission Guidelines
  • Please submit the datasets collected during the respective expedition along with metadata.
  • The datasets include observational data from the Arctic, Antarctic, Southern Ocean, and Himalaya spanning both short-term and long-term periods.
  • These datasets will be properly archived with corresponding metadata and the project you have participated.
  • Our NPDC is technical data repository for the Polar Datasets under Scientific Committee on Antarctic Research (SCAR) — Standing Committee on Antarctic Data Management (SCADM).
